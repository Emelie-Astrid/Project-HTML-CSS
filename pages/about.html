ABOUT

HISTORY

Tokyo Tours was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate individuals with a love for Tokyo and its many unique and fascinating attractions. With a vision of providing authentic and immersive experiences for foreign visitors, the company began offering a range of tours that showcase the city's rich culture and history.

As our reputation grew, we expanded our team of experienced guides and added new tour offerings, including food tours, technology tours, and cultural experiences such as tea ceremonies and martial arts demonstrations.

Today, Tokyo Tours is a well-established tour operator in Tokyo, with thousands of satisfied customers from around the world. We continue to innovate and provide personalized, high-quality tours that give our guests a truly authentic and memorable experience in Tokyo.
